WHO WE ARE?

"aK auberge, your friendly and hospitable hotel booking location."

Inspiring Touches For Rooms Uniquely Refreshing!
Creating something new…

For far too long, the hotel scene in India remains true to the tried and norm. There are the myriad of international 5-star hotels dedicated to serving trappings of luxury for the affluent visitor and for the business and leisure travellers, choices abound with a host of pragmatic and convenient array of value-for-money budget and 3-star hotels all over India. In the development of aK auberge as we remain faithful to the term “boutique”, the challenge was clearly cut out to deliver refreshing living concepts that will set apart the new establishment with its innovative ideas, dynamic pull factor and sustainable attractions.

The aK auberge, in its quest to spruce and liven up the Indian hotel scene, believes it has something unique to offer; nuggets of inspirational abodes you’ll probably thought only exist in Vegas or Paris.

Step into India’s hotel booking scene where thematic rooms merge “rest, experiential and memories” to unique levels as you keep company with iconic names in the entertainment world, rejuvenate your senses in different realms and partake serene, restful surroundings.
